What are the reasons why a person cannot donate blood?

There are certain conditions that prevent a person from donating blood temporarily or permanently. Among the temporary conditions are:-

  • Pregnancy
  • Recent alcoholic intake
  • Ear or body piercing
  • Acute fever
  • Surgery

Persons with the following conditions are also not allowed to donate blood anytime.

  • Cancer
  • Hepatitis B & C
  • Cardiac arrest
  • HIV infection , AIDS or sexually transmitted diseases
  • Chronic alcoholism

You donot have to be worried if you aren't eligible to donate blood. There is always a way around to help people across the nation . You can help us by being a part of our community. Our need for volunteers is constant and continues to evolve as we navigate this COVID-19 health crisis. Volunteer opportunities include supporting blood donations and delivering much needed services to your community.
